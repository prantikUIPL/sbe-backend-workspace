## Summary

Treat Check, Bank Wire, ACH, PayPal as manual payment methods; admins mark such payments Paid/Unpaid; logged and enforced server-side. At the 2026-07-03 gate this story converged to its least-work shape: **all four of its open design questions were resolved by the 24.8/24.9 gates**, and the write side is delivered by 24.8 —

- **Classification** (24.7-a): source of record = 24.8's `payment_type` column on payment_transactions (reusing `CartPaymentMethod` — exactly this story's taxonomy, `bank_wire_ach` covering Bank Wire + ACH; schema doc-comment: `credit_card` = Stripe card charge, the sole non-manual value). Backfill `credit_card` is provably safe (all 5 PT creation sites verified card flows at the 24.8 gate); no cart-plan fallback derivation needed. Subscription/ppl_addon orders are always-Stripe by construction.
- **Paid/Unpaid representation** (old OQ-1): RESOLVED — no new state; Mark-Paid flips `PT.status` to `succeeded` (+`paid_at`) and mirrors the webhook's money writes (`paid_amount`/`paid_in_full_at`/`computeNewOrderStatus`); the label is derived (`deriveOrderPaymentStatus` port). Neither of the doc's two original options (OrderStatus overload / new field) was taken.
- **Webhook coexistence** (old OQ-2): RESOLVED — manual toggling allowed **only** on manual-method installments, gated per-installment via `payment_type` (mixed card+manual plans handled cleanly); `credit_card` rows stay webhook-owned, no admin override.
- **Endpoint boundary (D3, decided at this gate):** the planned `PATCH /orders/:id/payment-status` is **DROPPED** — Mark-as-Paid/Unpaid lives solely in 24.8's installment PATCH (one writer, one audit trail, one permission surface; an order-level "mark paid" UI action = FE calling 24.8's PATCH on the eligible installment). **24.7 ships exactly one route: `GET /api/v1/orders/:id/payments`**, reusing 24.8's C13 mapper, surfacing the per-installment `payment_memo` (the order-level `Order.payment_memo` from 24.14 belongs to 24.6's aggregate — not duplicated here).

**FE-contract note (recorded at gate):** method dropdowns need no lookup endpoint — the enum is published via `@ApiProperty({ enum: CartPaymentMethod })` on 24.8's write DTOs (cart precedent `payment-plan.dto.ts:33-35`); the card-sibling constraint (manual `credit_card` add requires an existing Stripe identity) is documented on the DTO so the FE can disable contextually.
