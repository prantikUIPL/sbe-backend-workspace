## Summary

Adds the optional, default-checked "Send cancellation/refund email notification" toggle to the admin cancel (24.10) and refund (24.9) actions, enforced server-side. Re-verified 2026-07-03 on origin/dev@0ac2769: the dispatch primitive is live and proven (admin `MailerService.sendFromTemplate` at `mailer.service.ts:192` — per-notification_type template resolution, SendGrid send, `notification_logs` write :242, deliberately non-throwing) and the `send_notification` flag is already provisioned in both owning DTOs (locked at the 24.9/24.10 gates, default true).

**Decisions locked 2026-07-03:** (D1) **two trigger events** — working names `order_canceled` / `order_refunded` — with the **one-email-per-action rule**: cancel sends `order_canceled` (its token set includes optional refund fields), the standalone refund endpoint sends `order_refunded`; never both from one action. (D2) **24.11 seeds its own slugs + templates** (2 trigger_events with placeholder sets + 2 templates with TEMPLATE_META entries) — per the project-wide ownership rule confirmed at this gate: *every email flow owns its individual templates; the Email & SMS epic only manages them*. SBE-671 verified **merged to dev** (PR #426; zero unmerged commits), so the old "coordinate with the unmerged branch" caveat is retired — what remains is naming consistency with the catalogue and known-issue #21 (custom template can shadow predefined on live sends — accepted risk; fix ships with the E&S scheduling work). (D3) recipient resolution = **`Order.billing_email` → Company contact email → skip + log** (billing_email is nullable, schema:1564; mailer degrades gracefully; `notification_logs` records the address used or the skip).

**All 5 requirements are Deliverable** (24.11-c upgraded from Partial — its missing artifacts became story-owned net-new work under D2). Agreed shape for the send path: dispatch **after** the owning `$transaction` commits (non-throwing mailer ⇒ a failed email can't fail the action; a rolled-back action must never have emailed).
